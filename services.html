Investor Pitch

Start boosting your business's need for greater amounts of capital aid by sharing your vision, mission, and goals for your small business to open investors in DIM! Micro-Loans

Loans can be taken by both businesses who are looking to get easier claims of loans or investors who are greatly determined at starting their financial journey, but lacks the amount of capital needed to start carving their professional path.
We offer a range of options to help you get started on your financial journey today, including:
Loan Type Loan Amount Interest Rate
Startup Loans $500-$3,000 7.5%-10%
